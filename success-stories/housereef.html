House Reef

Leading Provider of Scuba Diving Courses, Snorkeling Adventures, and Trips in The Red Sea. With a focus on safety and quality, They offer unforgettable experiences to explore marine life.

Checkout Project

1. Brief

They offer a range of services including scuba diving courses, and guided trips, Our experienced instructors and crew ensure your safety and provide an exceptional experience.

2. Client Requirements

I want a platform with which I can exceptionally present my company and change the global face of diving in the Egyptian regions of the Red Sea, and I can offer my services such as diving trips and professional diving courses, all in a professional way through this platform.

3. The Challenges

The platform should have included features that make it easy for users to learn about the company’s diving trips and other professional diving courses, and know the prices of all of that in detail, and the details of the trips from the time of departure until the return. All of this should have been explained professionally.

4. The Solution

EG Systems scrutinized the solutions received to put priorities in the right place, develop the customer’s vision for these services, ease of dealing with them, and know in detail what House Reef offers.

EG Systems also designed an interface that deals with mobile phones and large computer screens, and it has become easy to browse the site from any device, due to the ease of dealing with these services provided by the House Reef company.
